Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer free delivery within Calubian, Leyte and nearby areas. Same-day pickup is also available at our farm store.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept GCash, Maya, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for your convenience.

How fresh is your corn?

Our sweet corn is handpicked daily from our farm to ensure maximum freshness and sweetness. We harvest only what we can deliver within 24 hours.

Can I visit your farm?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to our farm store. We're open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Sunday visits can be arranged by appointment.

Do you offer bulk orders for events?

Yes, we provide bulk orders for events, parties, and special occasions. Please contact us at least 2-3 days in advance to ensure availability.
